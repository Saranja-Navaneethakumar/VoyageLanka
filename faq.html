Frequently Asked Questions

What is Voyage Lanka?
Voyage Lanka is a premier travel agency that specializes in organizing unforgettable travel experiences in Sri Lanka, offering a range of tours from cultural heritage to adventure and relaxation.
How can I book a tour?
You can book a tour directly through our website by selecting a tour package and clicking "Learn More" to proceed to the booking page. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), PayPal, and bank transfers for all tour bookings. Details will be shared during the booking process.
Can I customize a tour package?
Absolutely! We offer custom tour packages tailored to your preferences. Contact our team with your specific requirements, and we’ll create a personalized itinerary just for you.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour package. Generally, cancellations made 30 days prior to the tour are eligible for a full refund. For more details, refer to the cancellation terms provided during booking.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, we offer group discounts for bookings with more than five people. Contact us for special rates and arrangements for larger groups.
What should I pack for my trip?
It depends on your destination and activities. Generally, we recommend lightweight clothing, comfortable shoes, sunscreen, a hat, and essentials like a first-aid kit. Our team will provide a detailed packing list before your trip.
