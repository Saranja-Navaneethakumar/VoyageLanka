Travel Information to Sri Lanka

Languages in Sri Lanka

The official languages are spoken by Sri Lankan people are Tamil & Sinhala. English is widely used language. You can find all three languages or two in the sign and name bords.

Currency in Sri Lanka

The currency of Sri Lanka is the Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs are commonly in all over the country but cash are more useful especially in rural areas. Credit card also accepted. To exchange the foreign currency approach bank nera you is the best option or find the official money exchangers to avoid the scammers.

Ethinic Groups in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is diverse of ethnic groups including Sinhalese, Tamils, Muslims and others. This culture impacts the country's festival, cuisine, and traditions.

Food & Cuisine of Sri Lanka

Sri Lankan cuisine has a wide range of food which includes mix of rice, curry, seafood, meat, vegetables and tropical fruits etc. Hoppers (bowl shape pancakes), Pittu (Made with rice flour and shredded coconut), Koththu (Made with chopped rotti with curry, meat, veggies etc), Sambol (greated coconut) those are the special items you must try in Sri Lanka. You can find plenty of ranges for non-veg and veg-food in all part of Sri Lanka.

Health & Vacinations

Make sure you got up-to-date vaccinations. Maleria is not prevalent but dengue fever is especially in rainy seasons. Find ou more about vaccination on Travel HealthPro Website.

Time Zone

Sri Lanka time zone is IST (India Standard Time) which is UTC+5:30 hours. It takes eleven and half hours to travel from UK to Sri Lanka by flight.
